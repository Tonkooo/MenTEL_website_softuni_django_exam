We are proud with our history!

Founded in 1989 in Bulgaria by Georgi Ivanov, MenTEL emerged as a pioneering force in the nation's telecommunications landscape. From its inception, MenTEL was driven by a vision to revolutionize communication, connecting people across Bulgaria and beyond. With a commitment to innovation and customer-centricity, MenTEL quickly established itself as a trusted leader in the industry. Over the decades, MenTEL has continuously evolved, introducing cutting-edge technologies and services to meet the ever-changing needs of its customers. Today, MenTEL stands as a testament to its founder's vision, empowering individuals and businesses alike to communicate seamlessly in the digital age.
